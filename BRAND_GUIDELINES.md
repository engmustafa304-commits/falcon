# Brand Guidelines

## Name

Falcon

## Category

Enterprise Automotive Operating System.

## Positioning

Falcon is a premium SaaS platform for modern dealerships, automotive groups, and future multi-country automotive operations.

## Personality

- Simple.
- Elegant.
- Precise.
- Confident.
- Professional.
- Fast.
- Premium.

## Product Feel

Falcon should feel closer to a world-class operating system than a traditional automotive listing site. Every interaction should feel calm, focused, trustworthy, and efficient.

## Visual Direction

The brand language is inspired by luxury automotive precision and modern SaaS clarity. It should be original and must not copy existing automotive platforms.

## Logo System

- Text logo: `Falcon`.
- Arabic text logo: `فالكون`.
- Abstract mark: road-movement inspired, not based on a car, wheel, key, steering wheel, or speedometer.
